Productional

01
description

Visual elements used in stages and sets for performing arts, film, concerts and other entertainment or artist productions. If can also be productional design in form of creative the motion graphics to a music video, tv-productions, etc. It differentiates from all other type of graphic design, because it's mostly graphics functioning as overlays for a video. It's similar to UI / UX design because the designer needs to keep in mind that the user needs to get some info without making it disturb what the viewer sees and understands.
