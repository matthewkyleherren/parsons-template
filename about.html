01
hello

I am a selftaught multidisciplinary designer (based on curiosity) — working within the field of strategic business development. I strive to define and solve my client's issues, and optimize their commercial existence, by diligently focusing on creating a highly converting outcome, for my client's business and their user's needs.

Design is all about solving a problem — that can only be done with a strategic approach to every decision. I'm designing and developing based on breaking down business objections and user needs. This will always make a converting result that provides happy customers for your users, and a strong visual presence for your brand.

My competencies combine business strategy, digital development, and design, which makes me capable of creating truly innovative solutions for businesses to thrive in a competitive market.

02
biography

Asger Lucas Krause Søbirk is born, raised and based in Copenhagen, Denmark. He is passionately attracted to design in any formand specifies with the graphical part of the industry. You can say that his path to present is infinitely long — if hours of interest counts. In February 2017 he founded his company, to make his passion his path.
